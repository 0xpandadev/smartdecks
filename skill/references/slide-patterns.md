# Slide Patterns

## Required Slide Fields

Each planned slide should include:
- `type`
- `action_title`
- `message`
- `exhibit_type`
- `content_blocks`
- `visual_direction`
- `sources`

## Common Archetypes

- Title slide
- Executive summary
- Agenda
- Section divider
- Key message
- Current state
- Finding
- Data chart
- Comparison table
- Option scorecard
- 2x2 matrix
- Issue tree
- Value chain
- Process flow
- Before/after
- Operating model
- Roadmap
- Risk and mitigation
- Financial impact
- Recommendation
- Next steps
- Appendix
- Sources

## Action Titles

Bad:
- Market overview
- Competitor comparison
- Roadmap

Good:
- The market remains attractive, but growth is shifting toward mid-market customers
- Competitor A leads on price, while our proposal leads on implementation support
- A phased rollout reduces adoption risk while preserving speed

## Density

Use one primary exhibit per slide. Keep supporting points to 3-5. Move details, caveats, and long tables to appendix or speaker notes.
